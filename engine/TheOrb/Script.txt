[Terminology]
* The Orb - Round Quadcopter Ship with side doors and central corridor
* Drone Cam - Headless free-flying Camera Control script in Ghost Mode
* Stretcher - Carries neurology patient into ship connected with cables and pipes
* The Patient - Orb-headed humanoid on a Stretcher being looked after by the Pilot
* The Pilot - Orb-headed cyborg in prosthetic exoskeleton in control of the ship
* Control Panel - Two-tier desk covered in modular boxes with connection points for cables
* Patching - Procedure executed by The Pilot wiring underneath the Control Panel table
* Overheads - Control Panels attached to the ceiling as opposed to on the ground
* Engine Room - Space at the back of the ship filled with multiple Procyon racks
* Control Room - Space at the front of the ship filled with multiple Control Panels
* Procyon - Data terminal with tilted portrait monitor and fuse racks with Breakers
* Breakers - Power Control Modules that trip if they detect an overcurrent condition
* Docking - Procedure of attachment between the Drone Cam and The Orb's Overheads
